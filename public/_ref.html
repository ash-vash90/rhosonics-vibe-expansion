The measurement principle

Sound travels through slurry differently than through water. We measure that.

No gamma source. No license. No sensor in the process stream. Just a transducer pair and four decades of acoustic signal processing.

01 / PULSE
Transducer emits a 2 MHz ultrasonic pulse.
Mounted against the pipe wall — no probe in the flow, no wetted parts, no wear from abrasive particles.
2.0MHz
02 / TRANSMIT
Sound crosses the slurry column.
Denser slurries slow the pulse by a measurable fraction. Temperature is captured simultaneously to compensate.
48kS/s sampling
03 / ANALYZE
Time-of-flight resolves density.
Proprietary signal processing rejects bubbles, pipe-wall ringing, and entrained air. Drift-free over years.
±0.1% SG
04 / OUTPUT
4–20 mA, Modbus, or OPC-UA.
Drops into your DCS, PLC, or historian with no middleware. Or write straight to your cloud via MQTT.
